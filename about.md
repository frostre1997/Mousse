# Mouse on Android

A Geode mod for **Geometry Dash** that brings desktop-style mouse and trackpad support to Android devices.

## Features

- ✅ **Native Pointer** — A visible mouse cursor rendered on screen
- ✅ **Click & Drag** — Full support for click-and-drag gameplay (platformer mode)
- ✅ **Scroll Wheel** — Use scroll to navigate menus and zoom in the editor
- ✅ **Right Click** — Right-click acts as a secondary input (e.g., delete in editor)
- ✅ **Adjustable Sensitivity** — Fine-tune pointer speed to your liking
- ✅ **Resizable Cursor** — Change the cursor size from tiny to huge
- ✅ **Toggle Visibility** — Hide the cursor if your device has its own

## How to Use

1. Connect a mouse or trackpad to your Android device via USB OTG or Bluetooth
2. Launch Geometry Dash with the mod installed
3. A small white circle cursor will appear — move your mouse to control it
4. Left-click to tap, right-click for secondary actions, scroll to navigate

## Notes

- The mod does **not** remap touch events — it adds an independent pointer layer on top of the game.
- Works best with external mice, but also functions with Bluetooth trackpads and some stylus input methods.
- If you encounter issues, try adjusting the sensitivity and cursor size in the mod settings.
